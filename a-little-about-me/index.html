About Me:

Discovery & Connection

If there is one thing I enjoy most – it’s exploring creative solutions to interesting topics. Whether by branching out my day to day knowledge through evolving hobbies or refining my professional skill set through the adoption of new tools and methods – I’m always on the lookout for novel ways to grow and add value to the world around me.

As early as High School I could be found skipping breaks to restore an antique TRS-80 Model II: originally donated as discarded thrift it would ultimately find life hosting the band program’s music library database. While in College I joined a volunteer network in developing a unique computerized information management solution for a regional non-profit free clinic system; allowing the organization opportunities to attain grant adoption opportunities and other critical funding relationships.

Through these experiences and more I learned early an appreciation for the impact that problem solving in novel ways can have to meaningfully shape experiences. During my career I have had the privilege to contribute to teams internationally in the roles of code development, hosting administration, cloud management, media consultation, team direction, professional community growth and more. As I move forward – I am always keeping an eye open for new opportunities that can grow my skills even further and connect me to fun new relationships and creative challenges.

Interested to learn more about my work – or inquire about opportunities to directly collaborate? Shoot me a note!
