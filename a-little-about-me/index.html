About Me:

Discovery & Connection

If there is one thing I enjoy most – it’s exploring creative solutions to interesting topics. Whether by branching out my day to day knowledge through evolving hobbies or refining my professional skill set through new tools and methods – I’m always on the lookout for novel ways to grow myself and add value to the world around me.

I began what would ultimately become my professional passion with a simple curiosity for technology and how it contributes to the world around us. In High School I could be found skipping breaks to restore an antique TRS-80 Model II; donated to the band program as discarded thrift it would ultimately reawaken to host a music library database. While in College I joined with a volunteer network to develop a computerized information management system for a regional non-profit clinic – a step forward for the organization that led to data-awareness opportunities toward essential grants and additional funding relationships.

Through these experiences and more I learned early an appreciation for the impact that problem solving in novel ways can have to meaningfully shape experiences. In the years since, I have had the privilege to contribute to teams internationally in the roles of code development, hosting systems administration, cloud management, project management, organizational team direction, professional community growth and more. As I move forward – I am always keeping an eye open for new relationships that can grow my skills even further and connect me to fun additional creative outlets.

Interested to learn more – or inquire about opportunities to directly collaborate? Shoot me a note!
